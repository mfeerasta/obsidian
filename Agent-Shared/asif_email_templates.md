# Asif Hashmi's email format (learned)

Going forward Hermes sends these instead. Use `hermes_mailer.send()`.

## Common to both

- **From**: `Hermes (M agent) <hermes@feerasta.pk>`
- **Reply-To**: `meerfeerasta@gmail.com`
- **Auto-CC**: `meerfeerasta@gmail.com` (always)
- **Signature** (replace Asif's block):
  ```
  
  Regards,
  
  Hermes
  AI Agent for M. Feerasta
  hermes@feerasta.pk
  ```

## 1. Monthly SBL Markup email

- **Trigger**: monthly cron 5th of month (after markup-calc completes successfully)
- **To**: `mfeerasta@rupafab.com` (Meer's biz address)
- **CC**: `afeerasta@earthlink.net` (Amir) + auto-CC meerfeerasta@gmail.com
- **Subject**: `PER-ACCT-Documents for Calculation of CEO Sb and Family SBL markup for <Month YYYY>`
- **Body**:
  ```
  Dear FD Sb,
  
  Please see attached the family SBL markup calculation and reconciliation
  for <Month YYYY>. Bank statements used for the calculation are also
  attached.
  
  <optional: HBL 5999 bank statement is missing for <Month> — only if true>
  
  Regards,
  
  Hermes (AI agent for M. Feerasta)
  ```
- **Attachments**:
  1. `<YYMM>-Bank CEO Sb SBL-Markup.xlsx` (generated by markup-calc skill)
  2. Per-account Soneri statement PDFs for that month (10 PDFs from Drive)

## 2. Monthly CDC Charges email

- **Trigger**: monthly cron 5th of month (after CDC pipeline completes)
- **To**: `afeerasta@earthlink.net` (Amir, who is primary on CDC accts)
- **CC**: `mfeerasta@rupafab.com` (Meer biz) + auto-CC meerfeerasta@gmail.com
- **Subject**: `PER-ACCT-CDC Charges-All Accounts updated for the months <Month YYYY>`
- **Body**:
  ```
  Dear Sir,
  
  Please see attached the detail of all charges for CDC accounts updated as
  per CDC Statements received, up to <Month YYYY>.
  
  <optional balance status line — based on whether all accts have positive balance:
   "Balances are sufficient in the accounts." OR
   "Following accounts are short of funds: <list>">
  
  Regards,
  
  Hermes
  ```
- **Attachments**:
  1. `<YYMM> - CDC Charges-All CDC Accounts.xlsx` (auto-generated mirroring Asif's "Soneri Shareholding" sheet)

## Notes for both

- Asif uses Outlook conventions (extra blank lines). Mirror.
- He doesn't bold or color text. Plain text body.
- Subject naming convention `PER-ACCT-...` = personal-accounts category. Keep.
- He CCs Amir (or Meer) depending on whose accounts. Mirror that split.
- Phone signature is Asif's — replace with Hermes/agent line only.
