For pitch/outreach email drafts (the Archie business outreach), M wants them to **end at the call-to-action question**. A minimal brand contact line (Feerasta | hello@feerasta.ai | feerasta.ai) is fine as the signature, but **no closing pleasantry**: no "Good luck," no "Looking forward," no "Best/Thanks" sign-off, nothing after the CTA + contact line.

**Why:** M finds the closing lines unnecessary and has flagged "good luck" specifically (twice). Keep outreach crisp and confident.

**How to apply:** when drafting any cold-outreach email/pitch, stop after the CTA and the one-line contact signature. Do not append a friendly closer. See [[archie-sandbox-email]], [[verifiable-info-only]].
