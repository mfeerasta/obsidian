2026-06-18, M: "while we are delivering good items, it no longer goes with the ethos of a boutique firm." The site grew into a priced 7-line SaaS catalog (prices everywhere, stat bands, "see a live demo →" on every page, the 7-card grid) = high-conversion supermarket, wrong register for a boutique.

**Why:** the rarefied firms compete on MEANING not price/availability and say LESS. Hermès never advertises + withholds waitlist info (opacity>transparency); The Row shows NO prices, minimal copy; Berkshire's bare site reads "serious money"; Bottega anti-logo; LVMH "more dreaming than owning"; scarcity/selectivity/anti-marketing/restraint/white-space.

**How to apply (the plan, docs/brand-boutique-strategy.md + PDF):** TWO REGISTERS under one house, don't go full-Hermès and kill the SMB funnel. (1) Brand/front layer = boutique: restrained, NO prices, selective ("we take a small number of clients, by introduction"), quiet, vast air, one statement, anti-marketing tone, the house as a quiet INDEX not a sell-grid. Homepage + Sovereign + Bespoke get this. (2) SMB layer (Studio/Ascent/Ledger/Assistant/Pricing) keeps clear+priced pages but DEEPER, reached by those who look, not on the front step; premium lines read "by enquiry". Caveats: scarcity must be real; keep a findable price/yes for the SMB buyer; mysterious about price/process, NEVER about honesty/capability/legal (still disclose AI, no faked proof).

**Built:** a boutique homepage DRAFT, LIVE to feel at **feerasta.ai/quiet** (noindex, not linked, doesn't replace the live index yet). `portfolio-site/quiet.html`, self-contained, dark #0a0e15, Fraunces light, huge air, one line "Intelligence, kept private", the 7 lines as a quiet hairline index, "We take a small number of clients. The rest is by introduction.", single "Enquire", no prices/stats/demos. **Next if M approves:** promote quiet→index, restraint-pass Sovereign+Bespoke, move pricing deeper.

**DECISION 2026-06-18 (after M flagged ZERO clients, need outreach; docs/positioning-and-outreach-decision.md + PDF):** do NOT transition to quiet now. **HYBRID, clarity-first, earn the quiet later.** Research-backed: (1) for a brand with NO demand, supply-scarcity ("we take a few clients") reads EMPTY not exclusive, you need demand-based proof first; premium/mystery only lands once familiar, so /quiet is phase-3 at phase-0 = PARK IT (keep the file, don't make it the front door). (2) Price transparency = 2026 trust standard, matters MORE for a no-name firm; KEEP the "from $X" pricing. (3) Competitors: Podium/Birdeye HIDE price (force sales call, can afford to, brand+salesforce) ~$299-999; Smith.ai/Rosie/Goodcall SHOW ($49-300). We copy neither, our better middle = **transparent "from" pricing + visible LIVE proof (the agent/scanner, our substitute for testimonials) + premium-RESTRAINED look (premium≠mysterious: cut stat-bands/"see a demo→"/eager arrows, add air, keep price+proof) + the free-scan/audit as the cold-outreach wedge**. (4) The real lever = OUTREACH not redesign: first 3 clients at a founding discount for testimonials+case study, warm/local first (Chiado, Bilal Bay Ridge), run free-scan outreach on the built lists (Chiado-76, Bay Ridge), 20-50 touches/wk + follow-ups, then bank proof. Mantra: "you earn the right to be mysterious; clarity now buys the silence later."
