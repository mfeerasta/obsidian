Researched 2026-06-13 (M scoping a Portugal arm; lives in Chiado, has NIF + apartment, see [[portugal-presence]]). Verdict: FEASIBLE, Chiado is a strong launch zone. Doc: `docs/portugal-feasibility.md` + PDF. See [[cost-breakdown]], [[pitch-rework-ai-first]], [[sovereign-intelligence]].

**Same as NA:** full stack works (Twilio +351, ElevenLabs, Lovable, Stripe, agent templates). Cold B2B email workable under GDPR legitimate-interest (~CASL-like). COGS ~identical.

**Key EU differences:** (1) **EU AI Act Article 50: mandatory "you're speaking to AI" disclosure, enforced Aug 2 2026, CNPD regulator, GDPR 4%-turnover fines** (we already disclose by choice). (2) Voice MUST be **European Portuguese pt-PT**, not Brazilian (verify ElevenLabs voice). (3) Payments CHEAPER: EU cards ~1.5%+€0.25 vs NA 2.9%+$0.30; SEPA Direct Debit 0.8% for retainers; must support Multibanco + MB WAY (Stripe handles both). (4) **WhatsApp-first**, not SMS (Archie has a WhatsApp bridge already); EU alphanumeric sender IDs often one-way so need +351 long code for two-way. (5) Prices 40-60% below NA: Receptionist €200-300, text-back €100-150, site €900-1,200: volume+presence play, not high-ticket. Margins hold (cheaper card fees offset).

**Barriers SOLVED by M's NIF + apartment:** Twilio needs a PT address for +351 (has it); invoicing PT clients = recibos verdes sole-trader on the NIF, no company needed (IVA 23% above €15k turnover; B2B reverse charge for VIES clients; confirm with a contabilista).

**Chiado scan:** density NOT a constraint (200+ restaurants/bars in Bairro Alto/Chiado + salons/wellness/boutique hotels/retail in a 15-min walk). 81% foreign-tourist-dependent, US #1 source, English is the commerce language → **multilingual receptionist is a strong pitch**. Segment nuance: restaurants already on TheFork/OpenTable → sell missed-call/after-hours/no-show/multilingual/reviews NOT basic booking; salons/wellness mixed (several FB-only) → booking widget lands hard; hotels OTA-dependent tiny front desks → after-hours capture; dental/aesthetic verified clinics sit slightly NORTH of Chiado (Marquês/Av. Liberdade), widen radius. Local AI shops (Visor.ai/Betalyra/Digiton) do consulting not the packaged SMB bundle: lane open; only 11.5% PT firms use AI.

**Launch path:** open atividade on NIF → +351 number + WhatsApp Business API → one pt-PT demo agent w/ Article 50 disclosure → M walks into 5-10 scanned Chiado businesses IN PERSON (home-turf = the whole edge, and EU law rewards warm/local over cold) → bill EUR via Stripe (SEPA for retainers). Risks: price ceiling real (€100-200/mo model, keep founder time low via templates), tight GDPR/CNPD compliance from day 1, tourism seasonality (spring-summer spike).
