Built 2026-06-11 after Bilal's NY website-first pitches kept bouncing. M's hypothesis (pitch AI's business impact first, website as the full-circle close) validated by 3 research agents. Full doc: `docs/pitch-rework-ai-first.md` + PDF in Downloads. See [[ai-employee-caio-playbook]], [[website-business]], [[bilal-bayridge-demos]].

**Autopsy (why website-first dies):** wrong category (filed under "website guys" vs Wix/$17 + nephew), worst value-equation product first, free pre-built demo = $0 anchor + zero-curiosity signal (Enns violation, unsolicited-redesign insult), gain-framing to status-quo-biased owners (loss-framing is 70%+ more effective per Corporate Visions), buy-now ask from a stranger (cold walk-ins 2-5%).

**New motion:** mystery-call targets BEFORE the door (unclaimed script, $600M mystery-shopping industry proves the mechanic) → Voss door open ("You're probably thinking I'm another website guy... Would it be crazy to take 90 seconds on the calls you're missing?") → "I called this shop yesterday at 2:10, it rang out" → live demo line at the counter → ask = Missed Revenue Audit or waiting list, NEVER buy-now → grand-slam offer "[Trade] Never-Miss-A-Call System" (live 48h, 10-captured-calls-in-30-days-or-free guarantee, 5/neighborhood + one-per-trade caps, month-to-month, website = BONUS one-pager in the stack) → day 30-60 full circle: recovered-calls report in hand, upsell Studio ("the serious shops give it a real home").

**Language rules:** never lead with "AI receptionist": say "$10-a-day employee," "missed-call insurance," "night-shift front desk." AI vs VOICEMAIL never vs staff. "Even Yelp sells this now" (Yelp Receptionist Oct 2025) as trust transfer. FTC v. Air AI (Aug 2025) = the scam shadow: de-risk every pitch (live demo, month-to-month, local name, honest disclosure) or AI-led triggers the scam reflex website pitches never did.

**Ops changes:** rebuild Bilal field kit around the script (mystery-call log, audit one-pager, cohort tracker); live demo line = mandatory equipment (paid provisioning, ask M); audit = product ($99 standalone or free to applicants, credited); NY wedge pricing $199-299/mo text-back, $399-599/mo with voice; metric = audits booked per door-day, not sites sold. Pre-built demos re-gated: revealed AFTER audit as "already built and reserved, holds until Friday" (expiring slot, endowment).
