M's company is a **branded house** under parent **Feerasta Industries** (holding co; M owns `feerastaindustries.com`). Each division = **"Feer" + a solid one-syllable Anglo noun** where the noun says what the division does (the [[website-business]] arm). Convention chosen by M on 2026-06-05.

**Key unlock:** the `Feer-` prefix namespace is wide open — every `Feer___` checked was BOTH `.com` ($11/yr) AND `.ai` ($160/2yr) free via Vercel. Staying in the family gives every division an exact-match .com + .ai. (Contrast: ~280 standalone brandable names checked → nearly all `.com` squatted; both-free single words barely exist.)

**ARCHITECTURE DECISION (2026-06-05): selective anonymity / endorsed-hybrid.** Reasoning: some lines eliminate jobs → M wants distance from blowback to his name/family. But most lines DON'T (private AI = infra, websites/design = none, AI-OS = augments). Verdict reached together: blowback risk is small + scales with fame (boutique B2B = invisible to AI-job outrage), concentrated in 2 of the lines, and neutralized by framing; operational/legal risk is handled by LLC/holding structure, NOT by name-hiding; a founder name is a TRUST ASSET in local relationship-driven B2B sales. So → DON'T anonymize everything. Heritage house on the safe lines; neutral standalone "orphan" brands ONLY on the 2 job-touching lines.

**FINAL LOCKED (2026-06-05): heritage maison + endorsed descriptors.** The earlier `Feer-`+noun stack (Feerhaus/Feercrest/Feeratlas/Feervault/Feerstone) was KILLED — stacking the prefix 5× reads cutesy/pun-factory, the opposite of boutique/luxury. Boutique = restraint: ONE house name said with confidence + plain elegant descriptors. Precedent for this exact model: Google (house + Gmail/Chrome jewels + Maps/Drive descriptors + walled-off Waymo), Bloomberg (surname house + Terminal/News/Law descriptors), Marriott→Ritz-Carlton & Toyota→Lexus (spin a SEPARATE brand when the house name would hurt/expose the line), Salesforce/Accenture (house + descriptor practices). "Feerstone" was dropped because a jewel must BREAK the house pattern (Gmail ≠ Googlemail); "Feer-stone" only echoed it → leftover, not jewel.
