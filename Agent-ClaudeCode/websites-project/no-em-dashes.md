**Never use em dashes (—) anywhere** — not in emails, documents, drafts, chat, code comments, or anything M reads or sends. M stated this emphatically 2026-06-08 ("always remove em dashes, I don't want them anywhere") after one appeared in a drafted email subject.

**Why:** M's firm style; em dashes read as off / not his voice. This is stricter than the global CLAUDE.md note ("don't use until necessary, ask first") — treat it as an absolute: do not use them at all, and do not ask.

**How to apply:** use a comma, colon, period, or parentheses instead. When drafting anything for M (esp. outbound emails / deliverables), scan for em dashes before showing/sending and remove them. En dashes and hyphens for ranges are fine; the banned character is the em dash.
